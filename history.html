History

Episode 1: Adventure

The story begins in a small town, fortified by cobblestone walls & kept safe by the Militia. Tyler enjoyed luxury in a small castle, previously his brother's who was a high ranking Militia official before he disappeared. One day Tyler stumbled upon Granny Bacon's secret underground base, & that's when this adventure truly began. Bacon was apart of the Rebels, who are working to overthrow the now tyranical Militia & uncover Ozai's ulterior motives. Tyler finds out Ryan disappeared after coming close to learning the truth, & joins Samantha in order to find the truth. His involvement & Militia framing him for a warehouse explosion makes him enemy number one. He also stumbles across ruins that has a seemingly useless staff. The Militia catch wind of the Rebels base of operations and blow up the Port City which Tyler/Samantha survive by hiding in the fountain. After building the new Tree Fortress Rebel Base in the port's ruins, Samantha finds out they're looking for something called an Earth Element. Tyler & Billy was tasked to finding out more...

Episode 2: Into the Void

Tyler/Billy begin their search for the Earth Element, at an ancient Dwarven City called Stoneholm. The city is closed off to outsiders & has been on lockdown for years. The reason for the lockdown is the Dwarven King has built a machine that can break through bedrock, finding a deep hidden core underneath the city. They sneak in and search the city. There they meet Baldwuf, revealed to be an elemental bender. Tyler takes the Earth Element before he can, & escapes. Back at the town, Ben asks to come on their journey & Tyler welcomes him. He also finds out Juno is the Water Bender, who gives him the water tablet. Tyler meets a mysterious figure called Val Petri, who tells him about a dangerous dungeon that he needs to explore. Tyler & Billy trek across an ancient ruin that has them making leaps of faith across the void itself, a seemingly endless pit of emptiness that resides beneath the bedrock. At the end they find a pile of Ender Pearls, which Tyler buries underneath his home for safe keeping.

Episode 3: Prison Break

Billy, Ben, & Tyler note the disappearance of Sully, & eventually discover Margaret has been tormenting him. After a near violent confrontation, Tyler snoops in her house & find she sent a letter to the Militia & got him arrested. The crew go to free Sully only to find out Margaret had lead them into a trap, devised by Ozai & the Earth Bender who were working together. They take the Earth Element from Tyler, but don't find the hidden Ender Pearls. The three are sent to a Militia prison. THey meet Solomon who tells them more about the Militia. About how they go from town to town offering protection & eventually take it over. They weren't a small military force gone rogue, turns out they were a massive operation. Tyler figures out Ozai is the Fire Bender. They find Sully & they all work together to start a prison riot & break out of jail. Solomon dies during the riot & Sully says he'll stay behind to make sure the rest of the freed prisoners make it to safety. Tyler, Billy, & Ben trek forward.

Episode 5: Homebound

Billy, Ben, & Tyler arrive to the town to see its walls crumbled. When they go inside they see utter destruction as giant boulders & craters populate the now vacant homes. The group cuts through stone golemn monsters to find Juno at the bottom of her castle. She gives them the Water Tablet & tells them they have to go to Mid Summer City & translate it. They rescue Billy's family from the now destryoed amusement park & Tyler recovers the hidden Ender Pearls he stashed beneath his home. They gather on their ship & the Captain makes sail to Mid Summer City.

Episode 6: Shipwrecked

On the way to Mid Summer City they are caught in a vicious storm & are shipwrecked on a lonely island. With most of the crew dead & Billy injured, Ben & Tyler travel inward to find passage. They come across a tribe of natives who are quire familiar with Mid Summer City. It turns out the island is part of a slave trade & they are hiding out. With their help Ben & Tyler gather the necessary supplies to repair the ship.

Episode 7: Mid Summer City

Tyler & his crew arrive at Mid Summer City, an impovershed kingdom in the midst of revolution by the Red Baron. In this city on the verge of collapse they must translate Juno's tablet in order to find the Water Element. During their time there they discover the malicious nature of the King, who has an underground facility with human experimentation. Along the way Tyler rescues a boy named Roth from slavery. Eventually they find the Water Element at a temple & agree to help the Red Baron emacipate the city, who turns out to be Monkey. While trying to help the revolution, Tyler is captured by the King.

Episode 8: King's Justice

After a mock trial Tyler is sentenced to compete in the colosseum games until he is killed while a smug Margaret looks on. Monkey & the others work on a plan to get him out while Tyler struggles to survive. Eventually Tyler, Ben, & Monkey fnish their plan & the colosseum is blown up by TNT. War between Revolutionaries & the monarch breaks out, & the Earth Bender comes to kill Tyler. After rescuing Billy from the Royal Keep & killing the King, Tyler escapes with Billy, Ben, Monkey, & Roth.

Episode 10: Beneath the Crust

Rebel Scouts have discovered a sizable Militia base in Northern Agar which Tyler & Billy infiltrate the base & rescue many rebel prisoners along the way, including Granny Bacon amd Juno. Billy & Tyler discover the Militia's Nether facility where the Militia are draining the lava to find the Fire Element. Tyler goes in Ozai's room & discovers he & Ryan had battled here a couple years ago, & Ozai had left Ryan trapped in the Nether. A Lava Monster emerges & after Tyler defeats it, the Fire Element is dropped. Unfortunately it is retrieved by Ozai, who traps Tyler in the Nether by locking the gate to the portal. Tyler is trapped in the Nether for months until he stumbles upon a pigmen village, where Ryan had been for 60 years. This occured over 400 years ago in pigmen time, although this is not even 2 years in regular time. The pigmen help Tyler escape through a second portal, the one which Ryan used.

Episode 14: Final Flash

Tyler finds himself in Val Petri's 2nd flashback where it recalls a younger Tyler/Ryan gathering supplies for their airship. Younger Tyler stumbles upon the basement of his childhood home where he finds out one of his parents was the Air Bender & learns about how the Elemental powers work. When Ryan & Tyler leave on their airship they crash in the desert. The dream ends as they struggle to get out, as Val Petri warns that Ozai is in the End, which caused some of the End to leak onto the overworld. Tyler & Monkey go to the End leakage & battle the Dark Apostle. Monkey is injured, but they close the portal. Immediately after the battle Tyler is taken to the final flashback, a battle between the Rebels & the Militia from Ryan's POV. It shows the Rebels driving Ozai & the Militia away, allowing the Rebels to find a dungeon. Inside is the Staff of Val Petri, which Ryan takes. Tyler & Ryan reunite.

Episode 15: The End

Monkey rides off to tend to his injuries, not before Ryan tells him he must "find the other side of the world". Ryan & Tyler torwards the End Portal which is being guarded by the Earth Bender who wants their Ender Pearls & Elements to get into the End. Tyler is almost killed by the Earth Bender until he activates his Elemental state & destroys the Earth Bender. He absorbs Earth Bending into his bloodline & ventures inside The End with Ryan. There he battles Ozai, who he defeats by making the Fire Bender fall into the void. They get to the Dark Prophet & find out he has already been awakened & will return to the overworld in 30 years. But they are able to bring back three souls & they revive Ben, Arnold, & Samantha. To escape The End they must defeat the Ender Dragon. Tyler & Ryan look at each other one last time before plunging into the portal, unaware of what awaits them on the other side.
