History

Episode 1: Adventure

The story begins in a small town, fortified by cobblestone walls and kept safe by the Militia. Tyler enjoyed luxury in a small castle, previously his brother's who was a high ranking Militia official before he disappeared. One day Tyler stumbled upon Granny Bacon's secret underground base, and that's when this adventure truly began. Bacon was apart of the Rebels, who are working to overthrow the now tyranical Militia and uncover Ozai's ulterior motives. Tyler finds out Ryan disappeared after coming close to learning the truth, and joins Samantha in order to find the truth. His involvement and Militia framing him for a warehouse explosion makes him enemy number one. He also stumbles across ruins that has a seemingly useless staff. The Militia catch wind of the Rebels base of operations and blow up the Port City which Tyler/Samantha survive by hiding in the fountain. After building the new Tree Fortress Rebel Base in the port's ruins, Samantha finds out they're looking for something called an Earth Element. Tyler and Billy was tasked to finding out more...

Episode 2: Into the Void

Tyler/Billy begin their search for the Earth Element, and find a tablet in the library that points to an ancient Dwarven City called Stoneholm. After given a lift by the Steampunk Crew, they arrive to find a city in dismay. The city is closed off to outsiders and has been on lockdown for years, which forces the duo to sneak in. The reason for the lockdown is the Dwarven King has built a machine that can break through the previously unbreakable bedrock, finding a deep hidden core underneath the city. There they meet Baldwuf, revealed to be an elemental bender. Tyler takes the Earth Element before he can, and escapes. Back at the town, Ben asks to help him on their journey and Tyler welcomes him aboard the next voyage. He also finds out Juno is the Water Bender, who gives himm the water tablet whichh she needs translated. Tyler meets a mysterious figure called Val Petri, who tells him about a dangerous dungeon that he needs to explore. Tyler and Billy trek across an ancient ruin that has them making leaps of faith across the void itself, a seemingly endless pit of emptiness that resides beneath the bedrock. At the end they find a pile of Ender Pearls, which Tyler buries underneath his home for safe keeping.

Episode 3: Prison Break

Billy, Ben, and Tyler note the disappearance of Sully, and eventually discover Margaret has been tormenting him. After confronting her at Ronnie's church and almost killing her, Tyler snoops in he house and find she has sent a letter to the Militia and gotten him arrested. The crew go to free Sully from this "secret" cell to find the ground disappear underneath their feet. Margaret had lead them into a trap, devised by Ozai and the Earth Bender who were working together. They take the Earth Element from Tyler, but don't find the hidden Ender Pearls. The three are sent to a Militia prison, where all the Militia's enemies are thrown. One of which being Solomon who tells them more about the Militia. About how they go from town to town offering protection and eventually take it over. They weren't a small military force gone rogue, turns out they were a massive operation. Tyler figures out Ozai is the Fire Bender. They find Sully and they all work together to start a prison riot and break out of jail. Solomon dies during the riot and Sully says he'll stay behind to make sure the rest of the freed prisoners make it to safety. Tyler, Billy, and Ben trek forward, back to their home.

Episode 5: Homebound

Billy, Ben, and Tyler arrive to the town to see its walls crumbled. When they go inside they see utter destruction as giant boulders and craters populate the now vacant homes. The group cuts through stone golemn monsters to find Juno at the bottom of her castle. She gives them the Water Tablet and tells them they have to go to Mid Summer City and translate it. They rescue Billy's family from the now destryoed amusement park and Tyler recovers the hidden Ender Pearls he stashed beneath his home. They gather on their ship and the Captain makes sail to Mid Summer City.

Episode 6: Shipwrecked

On the way to Mid Summer City they are caught in a vicious storm and are shipwrecked on a lonely island. With most of the crew dead and Billy injured, Ben and Tyler travel inward to find passage. They come across a tribe of natives who are quire familiar with Mid Summer City. It turns out the island is part of a slave trade and they are hiding out. With their help Ben and Tyler gather the necessary supplies to repair the ship.

Episode 7: Mid Summer City

Tyler and his crew arrive at Mid Summer City, an impovershed kingdom in the midst of revolution by the Red Baron. In this city on the verge of collapse they must translate Juno's tablet in order to find the Water Element. During their time there they discover the malicious nature of the King, who has an underground facility with human experimentation. Along the way Tyler rescues a boy named Roth from slavery. Eventually they find the Water Element at a temple and agree to help the Red Baron emacipate the city, who turns out to be Monkey. While trying to help the revolution, Tyler is captured by the King.

Episode 8: King's Justice

After a mock trial Tyler is sentenced to compete in the colosseum games until he is killed while a smug Margaret looks on. Monkey and the others work on a plan to get him out while Tyler struggles to survive. Eventually Tyler, Ben, and Monkey fnish their plan and the colosseum is blown up by TNT. War between Revolutionaries and the monarch breaks out, and the Earth Bender comes to kill Tyler. After rescuing Billy from the Royal Keep and killing the King, Tyler escapes with Billy, Ben, Monkey, and Roth.

Episode 10: Beneath the Crust

Rebel Scouts have discovered a sizable Militia base in Northern Agar which Tyler and Billy infiltrate the base and rescue many rebel prisoners along the way, including Granny Bacon amd Juno. Billy and Tyler discover the Militia's Nether facility where the Militia are draining the lava to find the Fire Element. Tyler goes in Ozai's room and discovers he and Ryan had battled here a couple years ago, and Ozai had left Ryan trapped in the Nether. A Lava Monster emerges and after Tyler defeats it, the Fire Element is dropped. Unfortunately it is retrieved by Ozai, who traps Tyler in the Nether by locking the gate to the portal. Tyler is trapped in the Nether for months until he stumbles upon a pigmen village, where Ryan had been for 60 years. This occured over 400 years ago in pigmen time, although this is not even 2 years in regular time. The pigmen help Tyler escape through a second portal, the one which Ryan used.

Episode 14: Final Flash

Tyler finds himself in Val Petri's 2nd flashback where it recalls a younger Tyler/Ryan gathering supplies for their airship. Younger Tyler stumbles upon the basement of his childhood home where he finds out one of his parents was the Air Bender and learns about how the Elemental powers work. When Ryan and Tyler leave on their airship they crash in the desert. The dream ends as they struggle to get out, as Val Petri warns that Ozai is in the End, which caused some of the End to leak onto the overworld. Tyler and Monkey go to the End leakage and battle the Dark Apostle. Monkey sustains heavy injuries but they close the portal. Immediately after the battle Tyler is taken to the final flashback, a battle between the Rebels and the Militia from Ryan's POV. It shows the Rebels driving Ozai and the Militia away, allowing the Rebels to find a dungeon. Inside is the Staff of Val Petri, which Ryan takes. The episode ends with Tyler and Ryan reuniting.

Episode 15: The End

Monkey rides off to tend to his injuries, not before Ryan tells him he must "find the other side of the world". Ryan and Tyler torwards the End Portal which is being guarded by the Earth Bender who wants their Ender Pearls and Elements to get into the End. Tyler is almost killed by the Earth Bender until he activates his Elemental state and destroys the Earth Bender. He absorbs Earth Bending into his bloodline and ventures inside The End with Ryan. There he battles Ozai, who he defeats by making the Fire Bender fall into the void. They get to the Dark Prophet and find out he has already been awakened and will return to the overworld in 30 years. But they are able to bring back three souls and they revive Ben, Arnold, and Samantha. To escape The End they must defeat the Ender Dragon. Tyler and Ryan look at each other one last time before plunging into the portal, unaware of what awaits them on the other side.
